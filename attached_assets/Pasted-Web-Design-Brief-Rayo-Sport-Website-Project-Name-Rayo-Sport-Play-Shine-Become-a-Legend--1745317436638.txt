Web Design Brief: Rayo Sport Website

🔹 Project Name: Rayo Sport – Play. Shine. Become a Legend.

🔹 Website Type: Static website (landing page + sections)

🔹 Objective:
Create a modern, sporty, and engaging one-page website for Rayo Sport, a football (soccer) community platform. The site will introduce the Rayo concept, explain how the matches work, show rules and FAQs, and include clear CTAs to download the mobile app, join our WhatsApp group, or follow on social media.

🔶 Concept Overview
Rayo Sport is a community-driven football platform that organizes 5vs5 and 3-team rotation matches in Moroccan cities. Players book matches, join local games, get ranked by performance, and enjoy a premium match experience (uniforms, referees, photos, etc.).
The goal is to become the #1 platform for community football in Morocco.

🔵 Color Palette & Theme
Primary Color: Electric Blue #007BFF (backgrounds, accents)

Secondary Color: White #FFFFFF (text, icons)

Accent Color: Jet Black #121212 or Dark Grey #1F1F1F (sections)

Optional Accent: Neon Green #00FFAB or Red #FF3B3B for CTA highlights or team colors

Font: 'Montserrat' – sporty, modern, and readable
